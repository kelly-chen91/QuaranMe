Timer

Your Very Own Countdown Timer

Now that you have identified the steps needed to help yourself practice self-care, your next task is to set some time to take care of those activities.

How To Use the Timer

  1. Click on "minutes" and/or "seconds" and input the amount of time you'd to be on your timer. Remember to type the appropriate value in each box; if you want your timer to be 3 minutes, type "3" into the "minutes" section.
  2. Press "Start" to begin your timer.
  3. If you need a break from your current activity, press Pause. Once you are done with your break, press "Resume" to pick up where you left off, or press "Restart" to start your countdown again.
  4. Once you are done with your task, press "End" to reset your timer to 00:00.
  5. *OPTIONAL* While you are completing your task, please feel free to listen to a live stream of Lo-fi hip-hop by clicking on the video in your timer.
