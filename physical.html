Physical Self Care


Exercise

  • Ride a bike
  • Lift weights
  • Walk your pets
  • Jog
  • Play sports that you love
  • Do yoga

Hygiene

  • Take a shower/bath
  • Skin Care (i.e. facial cleanser, face mask, etc.)
  • Dental Hygiene (i.e. brushing your teeth, flossing, etc.)
  • Grooming (i.e. shaving, clipping nails, haircut, etc.)
  • Glam up!

Diet

  • Switch to organic foods
  • Hydrate yourself frequently
  • Consume nutritional & fulfulling food
  • Consume sugar, fats, and oil in moderation
  • Cook for yourself; refrain from ordering take-out often
  • Eat vitamins and other supplements
